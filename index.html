All your favourite video game dishes in one place!


Hello, we're Gourmand, your local video game cuisine restaurant. We serve meals from The Legend of Zelda series, the Fallout series, Stardew Valley and more!


Founded in 1994 by Danny DeFreeman, who had a dream of serving cuisine from his favourite video games, The Gourmand has grown into an international, multi-million dollar enterprise! Danny spent many months experimenting with different recipies to find the perfect flavor and most importantly, looks.
